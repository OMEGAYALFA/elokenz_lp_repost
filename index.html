A smart social media queue to get more leads everyday

We repost your best content to bring in more leads

Repost is an auto-refilling queue sharing your content on selected social media platforms with different statuses.

Import a piece of content

Your piece of content is imported in our database and displayed in your Elokenz library.

Select social media accounts

Choose the platforms your article will be reposted on. Repost takes care of your content’s promotion.

Create custom statuses

Create as many statuses as you want per platform, Repost will select a different one each time your article is reposted.

Analyze your reach & improve your content

See which type of content and which statuses work best to attract new customers on a given platform & improve your content to reach more leads.

More than a simple sharing queue

Repost learns what your best content is based on your audience’s feedback and it will share your best ranking content more often to attract more leads.

Benefits

Repost helps you save time on daily social media tasks and reach more customers by using your content at its full potential.

Save time on content promotion

Increase long term reach

Your content is reshared over time, multiplying your reach on the long run.

No more passive assets
